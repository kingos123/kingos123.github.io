Strategic Insight: Why Only 4 Tiers?

1. Eliminating Decision Paralysis

Behavioral economics shows that offering 6-8 price options causes "Analysis Paralysis," leading customers to delay purchase. By consolidating to 4 distinct choices, we force a quicker, more confident buying decision.

2. Physical Alignment

Meridian Hall naturally divides into 4 clear value zones: Center Orch (VIP), Side/Rear Orch (Premium/Standard), and Balcony (Entry). Creating 6 price points forces you to draw arbitrary lines across identical seats.

3. Marketing Clarity

It is far more effective to market a "Festival Lineup" with a clear "Entry Price" ($75) and an "Exclusive VIP" ($360). Granular price differences dilute the value proposition.
